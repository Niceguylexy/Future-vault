Frequently Asked Questions

Are assets insured while in your custody?

Yes — assets are insured subject to policy terms. We provide coverage options during intake; contact sales to discuss limits and endorsements.

How does pickup and delivery work?

We use discrete, vetted couriers and tamper-evident packaging with verified chain-of-custody. All transfers are documented and signed at handover.

Can businesses customize vault space and access policies?

Yes — we offer bespoke custody plans, role-based access, and enterprise SLAs. Ask about hybrid private vaulting for corporate needs.
